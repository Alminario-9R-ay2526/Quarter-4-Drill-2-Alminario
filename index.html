⚡ How to use the Heat Index Checker:

  • Enter air temperature in degrees Celsius (°C) (typical range: 10°C – 50°C).
  • Enter relative humidity (%) (0% – 100%).
  • Press the "Check" button to calculate the Heat Index (apparent temperature).
  • The heat index reveals how hot it really feels, plus safety status: Extreme / Danger, Danger, Extreme Caution, Caution, Normal.
  • Reference: based on NOAA heat index formula (converted to °C).
